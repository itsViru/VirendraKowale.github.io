03 / THE THESIS

What really drives EV adoption?

Mar — Sept 2024 RWTH Aachen × Numbat GmbH Supervised by Dr. Moritz Wollbrink (Numbat) and RWTH examiners

"E-Mobility Market Dynamics: Drivers, Regulations, and Infrastructure Analysis." A mixed-methods study of 25+ European markets — regression and correlation models on pricing and regulatory data, plus a Python location-ranking algorithm (Pandas, Statsmodels). The OLS model reached R² = 0.735.

The headline finding: home/wallbox charging access — not price — is the dominant driver of EV adoption. KPI recommendations were presented to senior management and decision committees. For infrastructure companies, that reframes the whole investment question.
